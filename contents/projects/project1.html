Elemental Nexus

Adventure Level • Blender | Unreal Engine 5

Elemental Nexus is a 3D Open world game which was originated by 4 friends while enjoying their meal.

•  Genre - Open world
•  Theme - Viking
•  Main Goal - The main protagonist Freya have to collect all elements ( Air, Water, Fire, Earth & Spirit ) to defeat the main antagonist Sirgun.
•  Story - This begins with a Dragon's attack on the character's village
•  Placements - Where all different elements will be placed, and their Boses.
•  Assignments - Every one was given parts of the world to desing.

My part was this - Bruster

Goal/Objective:
1. Go to the Abondaned Village in Snowy Mountains, which have a well located in the center.
2. Find all three parts of a broken key to the secret door of a cave which is inside the well.
3. Solve the Puzzles in the cave, defeat the guardian of the Air - element and obtain the Element.

Core Gameplay
•  Explore
•  Fight
•  Survive
•  Upgrade
