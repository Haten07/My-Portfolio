Genre: Side scrolling, Platformer
This Is a game where the player is made up of fire. The game starts with a player being chased by enemies in space and suddenly a portal appears and the player enters it and then crashes on an unknown planet.

Main Goal/Objective:
Map 1. Go in the depth of caves and collect Irons to repair the spaceship.
Map 2. Collect Fuel from a new cave.
Map 3. Go to the third cave to uncover the truth of random portals.
