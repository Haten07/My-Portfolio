About Me

Hi, I’m Kashif Shaikh, a final-year Game Design student with a passion for gaming and a dream to create my own worlds. I’ve always been fascinated by the way games tell stories, engage players, and spark imagination.

After years of playing, I’ve decided it’s time to flip the script  —  I want to be the one behind the games. I’m constantly flooded with new ideas and creativity, whether it’s designing immersive gameplay experiences in Unity or Unreal Engine, crafting 3D models in Maya or Blender, or exploring new worlds through AR and VR.

My journey is just beginning, but I’m determined to make games that inspire others — and I can’t wait to see where my creativity takes me next!
